Brigaderio

The tastiest and easiest brazilian sweet

Whenever someone tries the brigadeiro and I tell them how I made it, they're always so suprised by it! It really couldn't get any easier, and I'll show you how to make it!

Ingredients needed:

  • 140z of Sweentened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1/4 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1 Cup of choclate sprinkles

How to make it:

  1. in a pot over low heat pour these ingredients in this order
    1. Butter
    2. When butter is melted, add the cocoa powder
    3. Once they are both well mixed, add the condensed milk
  2. Stir softely and continuously until it has a good consistency (generally you can tell by once you stir, it doesn't leave any residue where you just stirred)
  3. Pour it to a greased (with butter) plate and let it cool in the fridge for about an hour
  4. Now, after greasing your own hands, take small portions and roll them into little balls
  5. Roll the now formed brigadeiro onto the sprinkles
  6. Bon Apetit!
