Recipe for Caldo de Pinto

When it's bone freezing cold outside, try this!

Somedays you just need that warm fuzzy feeling of a soup heating your body from the winter cold. While some soups can be hard to make or even not have much of a kick, I can guarantee you that the caldo de pinto not only will heat you up, but it will taste so good that you just won't be able to stop having more!

Ingredients

  • 1kg of boiledcassava
  • 1/2kg of pulled chicken(seasoned beforehand with salt and pepper)
  • A diced onion
  • 2 cubes of chicken boullion cubes
  • 4 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 3 garlic gloves minced
  • Salt and pepper to your taste

How to make it:

  1. After baking the cassava, with its water, add it to the blender, blending until its a uniform liquid
  2. Stir-fry until browning the garlic and onion
  3. Add the pulled chicken and the boullion cubes, mixing them well
  4. Pour over the blended cassava
  5. Add salt and pepper, and serve while hot

Traditionally, you'd also add to your bowl salsa, parsely, pork crackling, hot sauce and a loaf on the side to dip on your soup!
